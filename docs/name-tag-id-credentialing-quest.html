Name Tag ID Credentialing Quest

Welcome! This is your guided journey to set up your physical Name Tag and build your personal Source of Truth Architecture. We keep it simple and private. No technical steps required.

Step 1 — LNbits Account Setup

LNbits is your wallet manager. In Satnam, open the LNbits section to create or connect your wallet. You’ll use this wallet when your Name Tag is “Stamped”.

  • In Satnam, open the LNbits integration and follow the prompts.
  • That’s it — your wallet is ready for later steps.

Step 2 — App Installation

Install the Boltcard programming app on your phone:

Install on iOS (Boltcard Programmer) Install on Android (Boltcard Programming)

These apps help you prepare (program) your physical Name Tag.

Step 3 — Data Input

Open the Boltcard app and follow the on-screen steps. You’ll enter a few simple details that connect your Name Tag to your LNbits wallet.

  • Choose a friendly label (e.g., “My Name Tag”).
  • Paste or confirm the details your Satnam LNbits integration provides (when required).
  • Your secrets never leave your phone.

Step 4 — Name Tag Stamping

Stamping is the moment your credentials are registered onto your physical Name Tag. After the app writes the data, return to Satnam and tap your Name Tag to confirm ownership.

  • Tap your Name Tag on your phone when prompted in Satnam.
  • Look for the success message — then you’re ready to go.

Your Source of Truth Architecture

Your Source of Truth Architecture keeps your identity facts consistent and verifiable. Your Name Tag becomes a trusted, private way to prove it’s really you — without a central authority.

Custom Lightning Address Integration

If you already have a Lightning Address (for example, from your own node or a third-party service), you can keep using it with your Name Tag.

  • Find your LNURL-pay URL: it is usually https://<your-domain>/.well-known/lnurlp/<your-username>. Example: https://example.com/.well-known/lnurlp/alice.
  • During Name Tag setup, enter your existing Lightning Address (e.g., alice@example.com). Satnam will validate reachability before programming.
  • The programming app will write your LNURL-pay URL to the Name Tag so others can tap and pay directly to your existing wallet/node.
  • Validation: after programming, tap your Name Tag and confirm your phone/browser opens your LNURL-pay page and shows a payment prompt.
